RKOSHI PACKERS & MOVERS PVT. LTD.

At Rkoshi Packers & Movers Pvt. Ltd., we offer comprehensive relocation solutions designed to meet diverse customer needs. Our end-to-end services ensure a hassle-free moving experience from start to finish.

Residential Relocation

Moving your home requires special care and attention to detail. Our residential relocation services include:

  • Professional packing of all household items using premium materials
  • Special handling for furniture, electronics, and fragile items like glassware and artwork
  • Systematic labeling for easy identification and organized unpacking
  • Safe loading, transportation, and careful unloading at your new home
  • Complete unpacking and arrangement services upon request

Corporate & Office Shifting

We understand that business continuity is critical. Our office relocation services minimize downtime and ensure your operations resume quickly:

  • Pre-move planning and coordination with your team
  • Secure packing of computers, servers, files, and sensitive documents
  • Professional handling of office furniture and equipment
  • Weekend or after-hours moving to avoid business disruption
  • Setup and arrangement at the new office location

Vehicle Transportation

Your vehicle deserves the same care as your other belongings. We offer safe and reliable vehicle transportation services:

  • Transportation for two-wheelers (motorcycles, scooters) and four-wheelers (cars, SUVs)
  • Specialized carriers and protective covering to prevent scratches and damage
  • GPS-enabled tracking for real-time location updates
  • Insurance coverage for complete peace of mind

Local & Long-Distance Moving

Whether you're moving within the same city or relocating to another state, we have the expertise and resources to handle it efficiently:

  • Local shifting: Quick and affordable solutions for moves within Bihar and Jharkhand
  • Interstate relocation: Nationwide network ensuring safe delivery across India
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate your preferred moving dates

Professional Packing & Unpacking

Proper packing is the foundation of a successful move. Our packing services include:

  • High-quality corrugated cartons, bubble wrap, foam sheets, and packing tape
  • Special crating for delicate items like mirrors, glass, and antiques
  • Waterproof covering for protection against weather conditions
  • Complete unpacking and debris removal at destination

Warehousing & Storage Solutions

Need temporary storage during your transition? We offer secure warehousing facilities:

  • Clean, climate-controlled storage spaces
  • 24/7 security surveillance and fire safety systems
  • Flexible storage periods (short-term and long-term options available)

Loading & Unloading Services

Our trained staff uses modern equipment and proper techniques to ensure safe handling:

  • Hydraulic lift and ramp assistance for heavy items
  • Systematic loading to maximize space and minimize movement during transit
  • Careful unloading with placement in designated rooms
